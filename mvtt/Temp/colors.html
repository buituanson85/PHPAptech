Coolnerds HTML Colors

To specify a color in HTML, use the RGB triplet or color name. RGB triplets work in most browsers. So, as an example, to put an Aliceblue background on a page, you'd use this tag:

<BODY BGCOLOR=#F0F8FF>

Coolnerds also offers tool that let you mix your own colors, view color pairs, and translate color names to RGB triplets and decimal numbers. Use the decimal numbers only in Java applets and ActiveX controls that require decimal numbers.
